Learn the island rules before you set sail.

Use your navigation skills to move across the board, then decide whether to accept island power-ups.

  • Arrow Up — move forward toward the finish
  • Arrow Left — shift left along the map
  • Arrow Right — shift right along the map
  • Y — accept the power-up
  • N — decline the power-up

Keep the game screen active and use the keyboard after your roll.

Island Tip: Power-ups can be powerful, but only if you choose them wisely.
